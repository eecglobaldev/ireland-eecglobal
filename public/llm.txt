# GLOSSARY OF TERMS (DefinedTerms for Knowledge Graph)

## Irish Immigration Terms
- term: Stamp 2
  definition: Irish student visa permission allowing study and part-time work (20hrs/term, 40hrs/holiday)

- term: Stamp 1G
  definition: Post-study work visa granting 12-24 months employment permission without sponsorship

- term: IRP Card
  definition: Irish Residence Permit - biometric ID for non-EEA nationals staying 90+ days. Cost €300

- term: INIS
  definition: Irish Naturalisation and Immigration Service - government visa processing body

- term: ILEP
  definition: Interim List of Eligible Programmes - approved courses for student visa

- term: Critical Skills Employment Permit
  definition: Irish work permit for €32,000+ salary roles, fast-track to permanent residency

- term: GNIB
  definition: Garda National Immigration Bureau - legacy term for immigration registration

## Irish Education Terms
- term: QQI
  definition: Quality and Qualifications Ireland - national qualifications authority

- term: NFQ
  definition: National Framework of Qualifications - 10-level Irish qualification system

- term: CAO
  definition: Central Applications Office - undergraduate application portal
